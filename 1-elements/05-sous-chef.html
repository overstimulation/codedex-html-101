• 4 cups dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • 4 tbsp miso paste
  • 150g silken tofu, cubed
  • 2 tbsp dried wakame seaweed
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  1. Hydrate wakame in water for 5 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat dashi in a pot until just simmering.
  3. Add tofu and wakame to the dashi.
  4. Dissolve miso paste in a ladle with some hot dashi, then add to the pot. Do not boil after adding miso.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with green onions.

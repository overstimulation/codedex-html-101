Mili

Mili is a Japanese indie music group founded in August 2012, consisting of Cassie Wei, Yamato Kasai, Yukihito Mitomo, Shoto Yoshida, and Ao Fujimori.
Mili covers electronic classical, contemporary classical, and post-classical genres of music in Japanese, English, Chinese, and French.


Mili Official Website

  • Cassie Wei or momocashew – vocalist, main lyricist
  • Yamato Kasai or HAMO – main composer, main arranger, sub lyricist, guitarist
  • Yukihito Mitomo – bassist
  • Shoto Yoshida – drummer
  • Ao Fujimori – illustrator, animator
